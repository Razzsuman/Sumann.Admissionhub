Dental Admission Process in Bangalore

Management Quota Admission

Management quota seats are reserved by the dental college management. These seats are filled at the discretion of the management and offer a direct path to admission. Students can apply directly to colleges or through consultants like us to secure these seats.

NEET Qualification

While management quota admissions may not require high NEET scores, candidates generally need to have appeared for NEET and secured at least the minimum qualifying marks. The exact requirements vary by college and year.

Required Documents

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • NEET scorecard
  • Transfer certificate
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Character certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • ID proof (Aadhar card, passport, etc.)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Career Prospects

A BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) degree opens various career paths:

  • Private dental practice
  • Hospital dentistry
  • Academic careers in dental colleges
  • Research in dental sciences
  • Specialization through MDS (Master of Dental Surgery)
  • Opportunities in government health services
