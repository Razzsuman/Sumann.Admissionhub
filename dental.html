Dental Admission Process in Bangalore

Management Quota Admission

Secure BDS admissions in 2025 through management quota seats, reserved by college management. Apply directly or through our consultancy for a seamless process.

NEET Qualification

Candidates must appear for NEET and meet minimum qualifying marks for management quota admissions. Requirements vary by college and year.

Required Documents

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • NEET scorecard
  • Transfer certificate
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)
  • Character certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • ID proof (Aadhar card, passport, etc.)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is management quota admission?

Management quota seats are reserved by colleges for direct admissions, bypassing competitive entrance exams. Our consultancy simplifies this process for you.

Do I need a high NEET score for BDS admissions?

While a high NEET score is ideal, management quota admissions often require only the minimum qualifying marks, varying by college.

How long is the BDS program?

The BDS program is a 5-year course, including 4 years of academic study and 1 year of internship.
